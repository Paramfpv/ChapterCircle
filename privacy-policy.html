Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2026
Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Chapter Circle (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Chapter Circle mobile application (“App”) and website https://chaptercircle.in. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is shared, and the choices available to you.

By using the App or our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically through your use of the App or website, and information from trusted third-party services. All user data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information such as email address and display name
  • Responses to onboarding questionnaires used to personalize learning recommendations
  • Messages and details shared via:
    • In-app support
    • Email communication
    • The “Contact Us” form available on contact.html

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data such as content viewed, watch time, interactions, feature usage, and preferences
  • Device and technical data including device type, operating system, app version, IP address, and crash logs
  • Analytics data collected in aggregated form to improve performance and usability

Location Data:

Chapter Circle does not collect, track, or store precise or real-time location data (such as GPS coordinates).

C. Subscription & Transaction Information

  • Subscription status, plan type, and transaction identifiers
  • We do not store or process credit/debit card details, UPI details, or banking information. All payments are handled securely by third-party payment processors (such as Razorpay).
  • Users may enable or cancel auto-debit subscriptions at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the App experience
  • Generate AI-driven recommendations and visual learning features
  • Enable notes, saved content, and community features
  • Respond to queries and support requests submitted via email, in-app support, or the website contact form
  • Manage subscriptions, access, and billing status
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and usability
  • Communicate service-related updates and notifications
  • Ensure platform safety, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations

3. AI-Based Personalization

Chapter Circle uses automated systems to personalize content, generate learning insights, and improve recommendations based on in-app behavior and preferences. These systems do not process sensitive personal identity data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or misuse your personal data under any circumstances.

All user data is stored in encrypted form and accessed only on a need-to-know basis.

We may share limited information with trusted third parties only in the following cases:

Service Providers

Including cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting, communication services, and payment processors (such as Razorpay). These providers may only use data to perform services on our behalf.

Legal Requirements

Where disclosure is required to comply with applicable laws or protect platform and user safety.

Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized or aggregated data that cannot reasonably identify individuals.

5. User Content

You retain ownership of the content you create in the App, including notes and “Circles” community posts.

By posting content, you grant Chapter Circle a limited license to host, store, display, and process such content solely to operate and improve the App.

We reserve the right to remove content that violates our Terms & Conditions or community guidelines.

6. Subscriptions & Payments

Chapter Circle offers subscription-based access through monthly or annual plans.

Payments are processed securely via Razorpay.

7. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal obligations.

Account Deletion

  • Users can delete their account at any time directly from within the App
  • Account deletion is immediate and irreversible
  • Upon deletion, personal data is permanently removed, except where retention is required by law

Support & Accessibility

Users may reach us at any time through:

  • Email: support@chaptercircle.in
  • The Contact Us form available on contact.html

We aim to respond to genuine support queries within 24 hours.

8. Children’s Privacy

Chapter Circle is intended for users aged 13 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal data. We do not access or use user data for any purpose beyond what is stated in this Privacy Policy.

10. Push Notifications & Permissions

The App may send optional push notifications related to learning reminders, new content, or service updates.

Permissions can be managed through device settings at any time.

11. International Data Processing

Data may be processed on secure servers located in India or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App or website indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
