Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chapter Circle?

Chapter Circle is a platform that transforms books into engaging video summaries in story format, making learning more accessible and memorable.

How can I access the video summaries?

You can browse our collection of video summaries on our website. Simply click on any book to watch its video summary.

Do you offer internships?

Yes! We offer both technical and non-technical internships. Check out our careers page for current openings.

How can I partner with Chapter Circle?

We're always open to partnerships! Send us a message through the contact form and we'll get back to you.
